Privacy Policy Review

Review a website or product privacy policy for the essentials that a privacy-compliant policy needs.

- The operating entity (company name, and address if required by jurisdiction) is identified
- The effective / last-updated date is stated and within the last 12 months (or the policy states it has not been updated and why)
- Categories of personal data collected are listed (name, email, location, device info, etc.)
- Purposes for processing each category of data are stated
- Legal bases for processing are stated where required (consent, contract, legitimate interest, legal obligation)
- Third parties with whom data is shared are named or described by category
- Data retention periods are stated (specific durations or explanation of the criteria)
- User rights are listed (access, deletion, correction, portability, withdrawal of consent, objection)
- A mechanism to exercise rights is provided (email address, form, or portal)
- Cross-border data transfer practices are addressed where applicable
- Cookie / tracking technology practices are addressed
- Children's data practices are addressed (age cutoff, verification)
- Contact information for privacy inquiries is provided
