*Form Completeness

Review this filled-in form — application, registration, intake, onboarding, government, or similar — and confirm every required field is populated, signed, and dated. Works on job applications, medical intake forms, lease applications, visa applications, enrollment forms, HR onboarding packets, tax forms, and any structured document with fillable fields.

- Every field labelled "Required" or marked with an asterisk is filled in
- Name fields (first, last, full legal) are populated and consistent with each other across the form
- At least one contact field is populated (email or phone); format is plausible
- Address fields include street, city, region / state / province, postal code, and country where applicable — no missing components
- Dates are populated, in a consistent format, and valid (no impossible dates like Feb 30, no dates in the wrong century)
- Numeric fields (income, amounts, quantities) are populated with plausible values and correct units
- Every checkbox group has at least one option selected where the form requires a selection
- Signature fields are signed (handwritten signature, typed name, or electronic signature mark visible)
- Every signature has an associated date that is not in the future
- Attached documents, photos, or supporting materials referenced by the form are present (e.g., "attach a copy of your ID")
- The form does not contain placeholder text that should have been replaced (e.g., "enter name here", "XXX", template field markers)
