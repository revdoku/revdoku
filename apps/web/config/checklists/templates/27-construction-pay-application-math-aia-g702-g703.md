Construction Pay Application Math (AIA G702 / G703)

Verify the internal math of an AIA G702 pay application summary and its G703 continuation sheet. Cross-check all totals between G702 and G703, row-level math on G703, and the Current Payment Due formula. Flag every inconsistency.

If the signed Contract Summary (with Schedule of Values and retainage terms) and the approved Change Orders log are attached, also verify that every Scheduled Value matches the contract's Schedule of Values, that G702 Line 2 (Net Change by Change Orders) equals the sum of only approved change orders, that the retainage percentage applied matches the contract, and that every change order referenced in the application appears in the approved log. Flag any unapproved change order or Schedule-of-Values deviation.

- G702 Line 3 (Contract Sum to Date) equals Line 1 (Original Contract Sum) + Line 2 (Net Change by Change Orders)
- The total of the G703 "Total Completed & Stored" column equals G702 Line 4 (Total Completed & Stored to Date)
- The total of the G703 "Previous Applications" column equals G702 Line 7 (Less Previous Certificates for Payment)
- For each G703 row: Previous + This Period + Materials Stored = Total Completed & Stored
- For each G703 row: % Complete = Total Completed & Stored ÷ Scheduled Value
- For each G703 row: Balance to Finish = Scheduled Value − Total Completed & Stored
- G702 Line 6 (Total Earned Less Retainage) = G702 Line 4 − G702 Line 5 (Retainage)
- G702 Line 8 (Current Payment Due) = G702 Line 6 − G702 Line 7 (Previous Certificates)
- G702 Line 9 (Balance to Finish Including Retainage) = Line 3 − Line 6
- No line item shows a completion percentage increase greater than 25 points in a single period without written justification
