Invoice vs. Quote

Cross-check an invoice against the quote it was issued under. Flag every deviation. #ref[Upload the quote] — the quote is required for this review.

- The invoice references the quote by quote number, proposal number, or date
- Every line item on the invoice appears on the quote (flag any added item not on the quote as an unauthorized addition)
- Every line item unit price on the invoice matches the quoted unit price exactly (flag any increase; flag decreases that could indicate a scope reduction not acknowledged)
- Quantities on the invoice do not exceed the quantities quoted (flag overages)
- Payment terms on the invoice match the quote (net terms, due date rules, early-pay discount structure)
- Any tax rate charged on the invoice matches the tax treatment stated on the quote
- Shipping, handling, and other ancillary charges on the invoice were disclosed on the quote
- The total on the invoice is consistent with the quoted total (line items × quantities, net of any quoted discounts)
