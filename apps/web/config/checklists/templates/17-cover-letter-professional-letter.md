Cover Letter / Professional Letter

Review a cover letter or business letter for the essentials of a professional document.

- Sender name and contact information are at the top (address, email, phone as applicable)
- Date of the letter is present
- Recipient name and address / company are present
- A salutation is present and uses an appropriate form ("Dear Hiring Manager", "Dear [Name]", "To whom it may concern")
- The opening paragraph clearly states the purpose of the letter
- The body paragraphs support the purpose with specific, non-generic content
- A closing line is present ("Sincerely", "Best regards", "Respectfully")
- Sender signature (handwritten or typed) is present at the bottom
- No placeholder text remains ("Dear [Hiring Manager]", "[Company Name]", "insert role here")
- Tense and person are consistent throughout the letter
