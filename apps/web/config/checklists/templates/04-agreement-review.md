*Agreement Review

Review this agreement — contract, SOW, order form, engagement letter, NDA, or similar — for completeness, internal consistency, and the presence of the essentials a signed agreement needs. Flag missing fields, placeholder text, inconsistent dates, broken cross-references, math errors, and internal contradictions. Also works for other structured documents (reports, specifications, plans, policies) with the same completeness lens.

If a reference document is attached (template, policy, prior version, job description, or similar), also verify that every required element from the reference is addressed, that no material terms deviate from the reference, and that the document does not introduce terms the reference does not support.

- The document includes all required identifying fields: title, effective or execution date, parties (or author / issuer and intended recipient where applicable), and a clear statement of subject or scope
- Dates on the document are valid and consistent across sections (effective date, term, signature date, any deadline or milestone dates — no impossible or contradictory dates)
- The document does not contain placeholder text that should have been filled in (e.g., "TBD", "[INSERT NAME]", "XXX", lorem ipsum)
- Every section referenced elsewhere in the document (in a table of contents, footer, or cross-reference) is actually present
- Numerical values are internally consistent: subtotals match their components, totals match, fee and rate cross-references align
- All math shown in the document is correct — addition, multiplication, percentages, and any other calculations
- Required signatures, stamps, or approvals are present where the document indicates they should be
- The document does not contain internal contradictions between sections (e.g., one section saying "X is approved" and another saying "X is pending")
- Terminology and defined terms are used consistently throughout the document
