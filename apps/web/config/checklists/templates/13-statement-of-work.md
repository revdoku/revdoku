Statement of Work

Review a Statement of Work (SOW) for clarity, completeness, and the essentials a deliverable-based engagement needs.

- Client and vendor are both named
- Effective date and expected completion date are stated
- Scope of work is described with enough specificity to be unambiguous (what is in scope, what is out of scope)
- Deliverables are enumerated as a concrete list with acceptance criteria for each
- Milestones and target dates are defined, with a dependency chain if relevant
- Fee structure is stated (fixed fee, time and materials, milestone-based) with amounts
- Payment schedule and invoicing cadence are defined
- Change request / change order process is described
- Assumptions, constraints, and client responsibilities are listed
- Acceptance criteria, testing approach, and sign-off process are defined
- References to the governing master agreement (MSA) are present if this SOW is issued under one
- Signature blocks are present with date
