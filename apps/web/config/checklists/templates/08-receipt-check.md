Receipt Check

Review this receipt — point-of-sale, restaurant, retail, or service — for the essentials needed for expense reimbursement or record-keeping.

- Merchant name is visible and readable
- Transaction date is present and not in the future
- Every purchased item has a description and line total
- Subtotal is the sum of line totals; tax is correctly calculated on the subtotal; total matches subtotal + tax + tip (if applicable)
- Payment method is visible (cash, card last four, or mobile wallet)
- Currency is identified if the receipt could be ambiguous (international merchant, multi-currency POS)
- If a tip line is shown, the tip is a plausible percentage of the subtotal (typically 10–25% for service industries)
- The receipt is not a duplicate or voided transaction (no "VOID", "CANCELLED", or "DUPLICATE" watermark unless expected)
