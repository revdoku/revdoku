*Resume Review

Review this resume or CV for completeness, chronological consistency, and obvious presentation issues that would get it rejected at screening.

- Candidate name is present and clearly displayed at the top
- At least one reliable contact method is present (email or phone); email is in a plausible format
- Work experience entries include employer, role / title, and start–end dates for each position
- Dates across work experience entries are chronologically sensible: no future start dates, no overlaps that are not explained, no gaps longer than three months left unaddressed
- The most recent position states either an end date or "Present" — no dangling entries
- Every work experience entry has at least one accomplishment or responsibility bullet (not just a job title)
- Education section lists institution, degree or qualification, and graduation date (or "in progress")
- No placeholder text remains (e.g., "Your Name", "[Company]", "Insert achievement here", lorem ipsum)
- Section headers are consistent in capitalization and formatting throughout
- No obvious typos or grammar errors in the candidate name, section headers, most recent role, or contact fields
- Tense is consistent within each experience entry: current role uses present tense, past roles use past tense
