Consent Form Review

Review a consent form — medical, research, photography, data processing, or similar — for the essentials that a valid consent needs.

- The consenting party is identified by full name
- The action being consented to is described in specific, non-technical language
- The purpose of the action is stated
- Risks, benefits, and alternatives (where applicable) are disclosed
- The consenting party's right to withdraw consent is stated, including how and when
- A signature from the consenting party is present with date
- A witness or administrator signature is present where the form requires it
- The form is dated and the date is not in the future
- No placeholder text remains ("[Name]", "insert purpose", etc.)
