Terms of Service Review

Review a website or product Terms of Service for the essentials a ToS needs.

- The operating entity is identified
- The effective / last-updated date is stated
- The acceptance mechanism is described (continued use, checkbox, account creation)
- The services covered by the terms are described
- User obligations and permitted / prohibited uses are stated
- Account registration, eligibility, and termination rules are stated
- Fees, billing, and refund policy are stated if the service is paid
- Intellectual property ownership (user content, service content) is addressed
- Warranty disclaimers are present
- Limitation of liability and damage caps are stated
- Indemnification obligations are stated
- Dispute resolution mechanism is stated (arbitration, class action waiver, venue)
- Governing law is specified
- Process for updates to the terms is stated with notice requirements
