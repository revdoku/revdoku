Investment memo

Northstar is raising $4.2M to expand its retailer operating platform across three new regions.

Why now

Independent retailers are adopting connected operations, but their inventory, customer, and delivery tools remain fragmented. Northstar replaces that stack with one system designed for local commerce.

Traction

ARR reached $1.8M in June 2026 with 142% net revenue retention. Expansion revenue now represents 38% of new ARR.

Use of funds

Product and engineering 45% · Go-to-market 35% · Operations and working capital 20%.
