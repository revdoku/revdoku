Q2 executive summary

Net revenue grew 18.4% quarter over quarter, ahead of the 14% plan, with activation improvements contributing most of the upside.

What changed

The guided onboarding release lifted activation by 5.6 percentage points. Repeat purchase also rose as lifecycle messaging reached more eligible customers.

Recommended next steps

  1. Roll guided onboarding out to the remaining customer segments.
  2. Launch the annual expansion offer for high-intent accounts.
  3. Measure activation quality alongside the 30-day retention rate.
